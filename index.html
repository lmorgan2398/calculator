Online Calculator

This calculator can perform basic multiplication, division, addition, and subtraction operations.

To prevent display errors, numbers entered or received (as answers) from the calculator may not exceed a length of 10 digits, including decimal points.

Answers will always be rounded to the nearest thousandth.

Calculations may be chained by selecting an operator instead of the equals sign.

The operation log will keep records of each operation once an answer has been calculated.

Keyboard inputs are supported.
